# Career HUB

Career HUB is a personal platform designed to manage professional opportunities, track job applications, build a database of companies, and maintain meaningful professional relationships over time.

The project was born from a simple need: keeping track of companies, positions, recruiters, interviews, and application outcomes in a structured and data-driven way.

Instead of being just another job application tracker, Career HUB aims to become a complete Career Relationship Management system.

---

## Vision

Most job tracking tools focus exclusively on submitted applications.

Career CRM takes a broader approach:

- Build and maintain a personal company database.
- Track job positions and opportunities.
- Manage professional contacts.
- Record activities and interactions.
- Analyze job search performance through metrics and dashboards.
- Create a long-term professional knowledge base.

A company can exist in the system even if no application has been submitted.

---

## Core Features

### Company Management

Store and organize companies of interest.

- Company information
- Location
- Website
- LinkedIn profile
- Remote policy
- Company status
- Tags and categories
- Personal notes

---

### Contact Management

Manage people associated with companies.

Examples:

- Recruiters
- CTOs
- Engineering Managers
- Team Leads
- Founders

Each contact includes:

- Name
- Role
- Email
- LinkedIn profile
- Relationship status
- Notes

---

### Position Management

Track available positions inside companies.

Examples:

- Backend Developer
- Full Stack Developer
- PHP Developer

Each position can contain:

- Salary range
- Source
- Original URL
- Description
- Publication date

---

### Application Tracking

Track every application through its lifecycle.

Pipeline example:

Draft
→ Applied
→ HR Interview
→ Technical Interview
→ Offer
→ Accepted

---

### Activity Timeline

Every meaningful event is stored as an activity.

Examples:

- Company added
- Recruiter contacted
- Application submitted
- Interview completed
- Offer received

This creates a complete timeline of interactions.

---

### Analytics

The platform will provide insights such as:

- Applications sent
- Response rate
- Interview rate
- Offer rate
- Most effective job sources
- Company distribution by status
- Technology trends
